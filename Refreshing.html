Preferably enjoyed by the pool

Energy-boosting Citrus Smoothie

Ingredients for two servings

  • One and a half oranges
  • One and a half lemons
  • Six spinach leaves
  • One and a half peaches
  • Three carrots
  • 500ml of almond milk

Method

  • Peel and chop the oranges, lemons, peaches and carrots
  • Remove the seeds from the lemons and oranges
  • Blend everything together until smooth
